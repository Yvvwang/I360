How To Make ChongQing Hot Pot

SERVINGS    4
TIME   1 HOUR 30 MINUTE
FLAVOUR  SPICY

Base of Soup

  • 1 Beef stick bone
  • Many Green Onion
  • Some Ginger
  • Some Garlic

To Do

  1. Put the beef bone into the pot, add in some water, boil for 10min
  2. Add the green onion, ginger and garlic into the soup, boil for 1hr

Flavour the Soup

  • Different type of Chilli
  • Mixed Spices
  • Beef Oil
  • Rock Sugar
  • Bean paste

To Do

  1. Use water to boil the chilli
  2. Put the boiled chilli into the food processor to smashed
  3. Use the beef oil to fry the spices and the bean paste
  4. Add water into the pot and then add the rock sugar

To Finish

  • Food you like

To Do

  1. With the sauce made before, add the beef bone soup
  2. Wait for the soup boiling
  3. Add the food into the soup and start eating

Learn more about the Hotpot

Hot pot is a traditional Chinese food which is very popular in China.
It has been existence in Chinese history for a long time.
Hotpot is an essential part of Chinese food culture.
Different area usually has a different taste of hotpot.
